Our financial record

As an open-minded NGO, we believe in transparency and accountability. We believe that it is important for our stakeholders to have access to our financial data in order to better understand how their contributions are being used to further our mission and impact.
We are committed to being a transparent organization and to sharing our financial information with anyone who is interested in learning more about our work. This includes providing detailed reports on our revenue and expenses, as well as information on how we use donated funds to achieve our goals.
We believe that by openly sharing our financial data, we are demonstrating our trust in our supporters and our commitment to responsible and ethical management of funds. We are proud to have a reputation as a transparent and accountable organization, and we will continue to be open and transparent about our financial operations in the future.
Our hope is that by sharing this information, we will inspire other organizations to follow our lead and prioritize transparency in their own operations. We believe that by working together, we can help to build a better world where people have access to the information they need to make informed decisions about where and how they choose to support causes they care about.

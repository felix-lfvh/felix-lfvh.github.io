Paid Work

Fundraising specialist

  • They oversee the activities related to individual, corporate and other donors for organizations;

  • They manage the relationships with donors, research opportunities for potential new donors and maintain records of donations;

  • For new donors, they may send emails, make calls or arrange meetings to discuss opportunities.

Advertising specialist

  • They manage the ad campaigns for an organization;

  • They evaluate an organization's current strategies and suggest times, locations and partnerships to help improve ad performance;

  • They oversee an organization's advertising accounts, maintaining schedules and budgets.

Canvasser

To share information, collect donations or engage individuals in the community. They often go to public places or visit houses to share the mission and goals of an organization. They maintain a positive and engaging attitude, write and recite scripts for the organization, and generate reports or lists of their results.

Program manager

  • They oversee one or all of the different programs our organization offers;

  • They create new programs or manage existing ones, including their budgets, schedules events and outreach.
